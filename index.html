The Essence of Padwa

🕉️

Creation

Celebrating the cosmic day Lord Brahma created the universe and the flow of time.

🔱

Victory

A tribute to King Shalivahana’s triumph and the auspicious return of Lord Rama.

🌸

Nature

Welcoming Chaitra Shukla Pratipada—the bloom of Spring and a fresh harvest.

Rituals & Traditions

  • ✨ The Gudi: A silken cloth tied to bamboo, adorned with Neem and a copper Kalash.
  • ✨ Neem & Jaggery: A sacred mix representing the balance of life’s bitter and sweet phases.
  • ✨ Puran Poli: The golden, sweet flatbread that defines the flavor of the New Year.
